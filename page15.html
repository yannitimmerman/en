THE TRUMP TIMES

PreviousNextClose

Design a 24-paged newspaper with the topics Ronald McDonald and Donald Trump. Take a specific critical opinion in this subject and develop a matching dingbat font. This 24 counting font will be digitalized in Robofont. Finally, except for your dingbat, only use Times New Roman and Neue Helvetica. The newspapers will be sent to and printed by: The Newspaper Club in London.

Interpretation:

The newspaper has become a comparison between Donald Trump and P.T. Barnum in which I want to reflect the idea of Trump who rules over America like it’s some kind of illusionary circus. Every page represents an ‘act’ in which rivals are pictured as freaks in his show. Both the dingbat font and the pictures in the paper consist of bizar elements wich can be found in optical illusions.
